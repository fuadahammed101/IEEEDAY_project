About IEEE DAY 25 at GUB

Join us for the IEEE DAY 25 celebration at Green University of Bangladesh (GUB), a prestigious event dedicated to recognizing the contributions of IEEE members and the transformative impact of technology on society.

This year, we are excited to host a series of engaging activities, including:

  • Inspiring Speeches: Hear from industry leaders and experts who will share their insights on the latest trends and innovations in technology.
  • Interactive Workshops: Participate in hands-on sessions that delve into cutting-edge topics and practical skills in engineering and technology.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow students, faculty, and professionals to build relationships and explore potential collaborations.
  • Showcase of Talent: Discover the innovative projects and research conducted by GUB students, highlighting their creativity and ingenuity.
  • Cultural Performances: Enjoy a variety of entertainment that celebrates the vibrant community at GUB, making this event a well-rounded experience.

We invite everyone to be part of this exciting celebration. Whether you are a student, a professional, or simply passionate about technology, IEEE DAY 25 at GUB promises to be an enriching experience filled with knowledge, inspiration, and fun.
